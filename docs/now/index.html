Updated on December 18th, 2025.

The last quarter of 2025 was hectic as well as eventful. I painted a lot, read a bunch of good books, met family and childhood friends, went on very nice walks, and found a new flatmate. I've deleted my Instagram account forever and joined Mastodon. Finally, I joined two feminist book clubs and am hoping to read more books on feminism and gender in the upcoming year.

Things/places/people I have discovered recently:

  • Two books I loved and cannot recommend enough: Why Loiter (Shilpa Phadke) and Brotherless Night (V.V. Ganeshananthan)
  • The Women and Gender Book Club is a monthly, online book club. Their YouTube channel has recordings of some meetings where they invited the author of the book they were reading.
