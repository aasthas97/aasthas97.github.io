What is this?

Reviewing the cafes I visit in Berlin. Most of them are in and around Friedrichshain. I usually order a single coffee and either a croissant or dessert. I am not a coffee expert, by any means. Nor am I a very eloquent reviewer. I simply enjoy drinking coffee and documenting my experience.

PS. I am also slowly learning German and will try to add translations soon. :)
